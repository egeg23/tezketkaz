# Privacy Policy

_Last updated: 1 May 2026_

TezKetKaz ("we", "us", "our") operates the TezKetKaz delivery platform that connects buyers, shops, and couriers across the Republic of Uzbekistan and the Republic of Kazakhstan. This policy explains what personal data we collect, how we use it, with whom we share it, and the rights you have over your data. By using TezKetKaz you accept the practices described below.

## 1. Who we are

TezKetKaz LLC is the controller of personal data processed through the TezKetKaz mobile apps, vendor portal, courier app, and web sites. Our registered office is in Tashkent, Uzbekistan. You can reach our data protection contact at **support@tezketkaz.uz**.

## 2. What we collect

We collect the following categories of personal data, depending on the role you sign up for:

- **Account data:** your phone number (used as your primary identifier), display name, preferred language, country (UZ or KZ), and the OAuth identity (Google / Apple / Yandex) that you used to sign in, where applicable.
- **Address and location data:** every saved delivery address (label, free-text address, latitude / longitude), the live GPS coordinates of couriers on shift, and the pick-up / drop-off locations of each order. For couriers, location is captured every few seconds while a shift is active; for buyers we only use the device location you grant per session.
- **Order data:** the items you order, modifiers, totals, applied coupons, loyalty redemptions, the shop, the courier assigned, delivery instructions, ratings and reviews left after delivery.
- **Payment data:** we never store full card numbers. For card payments we receive a **payment token** from Click, Payme, Uzum (Uzbekistan) or Kaspi (Kazakhstan) along with the last four digits and card scheme. For cash orders we record the amount only.
- **Communications:** messages exchanged in the in-app chat between buyer, shop, courier, and support; ticket text and attachments sent to customer support; OTP and login events for security audit.
- **Device and push data:** Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM) push tokens used to deliver notifications, plus device model, OS version, and app version for diagnostics.
- **KYC data (couriers only):** scans of identity documents, selfie, driver's licence (if applicable), vehicle photos, and bank account details for payouts.

## 3. How we use your data

We process personal data on the following lawful bases: (i) performance of the contract you entered with us when you opened your account; (ii) our legitimate interests in operating a safe, fraud-free service; (iii) compliance with tax, accounting, and consumer-protection law; and (iv) your explicit consent for marketing pushes and optional analytics.

Specifically we use the data to:

- create and authenticate your account;
- accept orders, route them to shops, dispatch couriers, and show real-time tracking;
- calculate delivery fees, surge multipliers, and courier payouts;
- run fraud and abuse checks (e.g. flagging accounts with high cancellation rates);
- handle refunds and disputes;
- provide customer support and answer your enquiries;
- send transactional emails and pushes (order updates, payment receipts) — these you cannot opt out of while you have an active account;
- send marketing pushes and promotional emails — only if you have **opted in** via `notificationPrefs`;
- improve the service through aggregate, de-identified analytics.

## 4. Third parties we share data with

We share the minimum data needed with the following processors:

- **Click, Payme, Uzum, Kaspi** — payment processing. They receive your phone, order amount, and (for tokenisation) the card details you enter on their hosted forms.
- **Yandex Maps and Yandex Geocoder** — for address autocomplete, route drawing, and ETA estimation. Coordinates are sent without your name or phone.
- **Google Firebase** — for FCM push notifications, crash reporting, and analytics. FCM tokens are not personally identifying on their own.
- **Sentry** — for error tracking. We scrub phone numbers and emails before sending; only stack traces, request IDs and anonymous user IDs are forwarded.
- **Resend** — for transactional email (order confirmations, refund receipts).
- **Tax authorities, courts, and law-enforcement bodies** — only where required by Uzbek or Kazakh law and only on a properly served legal request.

We do not sell your personal data to anyone. We do not show third-party ad networks inside the app.

## 5. Retention

We keep order records for **five years** after the order is completed, as required by tax and accounting law. KYC documents for couriers are kept until the courier account is deactivated **plus three years**, to support audits. Customer support tickets are kept for two years. If you delete your account (see section 6), we apply a **30-day grace period** during which you can restore it, then permanently anonymise the account; the underlying order rows remain for the five-year statutory period but stripped of identifiers.

## 6. Your rights

You can exercise all of the following rights inside the app or by emailing **support@tezketkaz.uz**:

- **Access and export.** Request a machine-readable export of all data tied to your account (Settings -> Privacy -> Export my data).
- **Delete account.** Request account deletion (Settings -> Privacy -> Delete account). We mark your account for deletion and finalise the erasure after the 30-day grace period.
- **Rectification.** Update your phone, name, addresses, and payment methods at any time in Settings.
- **Object to marketing.** Toggle off "Promotional notifications" in Settings -> Notifications. Transactional notifications cannot be disabled while orders are active.
- **Complaint.** You may complain to the State Inspectorate for Control in the Sphere of Informatisation and Telecommunications of the Republic of Uzbekistan.

## 7. Security

We protect personal data with TLS 1.2+ in transit, AES-256 at rest, role-based access control, and rotating credentials. We log all admin access to user data. We require all couriers and staff to sign confidentiality undertakings.

## 8. Children

TezKetKaz is not directed at children under 16. If you believe a minor has registered, please contact us and we will delete the account.

## 9. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy. Material changes will be announced through the app at least 14 days before they take effect.

## 10. Contact

For any privacy question, write to **support@tezketkaz.uz**.
